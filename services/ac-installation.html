Split AC Installation Dubai Service

Split AC systems are the most commonly used air conditioners in Dubai homes and offices. Our split AC installation service includes indoor and outdoor unit mounting, copper piping, insulation, drainage setup, and full system testing.

We ensure the outdoor unit is installed in a well-ventilated area for maximum efficiency, while the indoor unit is placed for optimal air distribution.

Central AC Installation Dubai

For villas, large apartments, and commercial spaces, central AC systems are ideal. We provide complete central AC installation including duct design, air handling unit setup, thermostat configuration, and airflow balancing.

Our experts ensure even cooling throughout the entire property with energy-efficient performance.
