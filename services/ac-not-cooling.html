Common Reasons Why AC Stops Cooling

There are several reasons why your air conditioner may stop cooling properly. Our technicians identify the exact issue before starting any repair work. The most common causes include:

  • Low or leaking refrigerant gas
  • Dirty air filters blocking airflow
  • Faulty compressor
  • Blocked condenser coils
  • Thermostat malfunction
  • Fan motor issues
  • Electrical or PCB failure

Each of these problems requires professional diagnosis and repair to ensure long-term performance.

Low Gas / Refrigerant Leakage Issue

One of the biggest reasons for AC not cooling in Dubai is gas leakage. When refrigerant levels drop, the AC cannot produce cold air properly. Our technicians check for leaks, repair them, and refill the gas using proper tools and safety standards.

We ensure your AC system is fully sealed and tested before completion.

Dirty Filters and Blocked Airflow

Dust accumulation is very common in Dubai due to weather conditions. When filters get clogged, airflow reduces and cooling becomes weak. We clean or replace filters and ensure smooth airflow for maximum cooling efficiency.
