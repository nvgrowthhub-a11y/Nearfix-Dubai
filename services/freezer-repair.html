Common Freezer Problems We Fix

Freezers can develop multiple issues due to continuous usage, gas leakage, or mechanical faults. Our technicians regularly fix the following problems:

  • Compressor not working

  • Door sealing issues

We perform complete diagnostics before repair to ensure the exact problem is identified and fixed correctly.

Freezer Not Cooling Issue

One of the most common freezer problems is lack of cooling. This can be caused by low refrigerant gas, compressor failure, or blocked airflow. Our technicians inspect the system thoroughly and restore proper cooling performance using professional tools.

Gas Leakage and Compressor Repair

Gas leakage is a major issue in freezers, especially in Dubai’s hot climate. When refrigerant levels drop, cooling efficiency decreases significantly. We detect leakage points, repair them, and refill gas safely.

If the compressor is faulty, we repair or replace it depending on the condition to ensure long-term performance.
