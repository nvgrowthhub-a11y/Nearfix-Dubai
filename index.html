Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

How much does AC repair cost?

+

AC repair cost depends on the issue and required spare parts. On average, basic repairs range from AED 150 to AED 500. Complex compressor repairs may cost AED 800-1500. We provide free diagnosis and quotation before starting work.

Do you provide service warranty?

+

Yes! We provide 30 days service warranty on all repairs. If the same issue occurs within 30 days, we'll fix it for free. Parts have manufacturer warranty as well.

Do you provide same-day service?

+

Yes, we offer same-day service in most areas. Call us before 2 PM for same-day appointments. Service availability depends on technician availability and your location in Dubai.

Are your technicians certified?

+

Yes, all our technicians are certified, experienced, and background-verified. They undergo regular training on latest appliance models and repair techniques.

Do you use genuine spare parts?

+

100% genuine and original spare parts only. We have partnerships with authorized distributors. Every part comes with proper warranty and authenticity certificate.

What if I need emergency service?

+

We provide 24/7 emergency service. Call us or message on WhatsApp anytime. Emergency calls are prioritized and technician will reach you ASAP.
